The Pillars of Tariqah Sheikh Ahmad Tijani & Faydah of Alhaji Ibrahim Niass

This page is dedicated to: The Tariqah Tijaniyyah wa Faydah Ibrahim, providing detailed information about the key figures in this esteemed spiritual path. It covers the lineage of leadership, starting from the founder of the Tariqah, Maolana Sheikh Ahmad Tijani, and follows the succession to his Khalifah, Maolana Sheikh Alhaji Ibrahim Niass al-Khaolakhy. The page also highlights Maolana Seyyidy Ali Cissé, the sole disciple of Sheikh Ibrahim Niass, who became the door of the Faydah and played a pivotal role in its growth.

Additionally, the page honors other prominent Sheikhs and mentors within the Tariqah, starting from Maolana Sheikh Imam Hassan Cissé, and moves on to the current Khalifah of Sheikh Ibrahim Niass: Maolana Sheikh Mouhamadou Mahi Niass, the Imam of the Faydah Imam Sheikh Ahmad Tijani Cissé, and finally to my own Sheikh, my mentor, my light Maolana Sheikh Mouhamdou Mahi Aliou Cissé, the spokesperson of the Faydah and current acting Imam of the Faydah.

You can click on each name to learn more about these esteemed figures. Similarly, you can visit the page owner’s profile for full access to detailed information about them. May Allah be pleased with them all.
